Welcome to the Pallets Projects!

The Pallets Projects are a collection of Python web development
libraries that were independently developed by Armin Ronacher and
later used as the basis of the Flask microframework. Today the Pallets
Projects are a community-driven organization with the goal to maintain
and improve those libraries.
